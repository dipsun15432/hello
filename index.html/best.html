Our Services

Foreign Employment Medical: We perform DOFE-authorized medical exams for people going abroad. All required tests are done in one visit.
Whole Body Health Check: Our health packages include blood tests, organ checks, and doctor consultation. Early detection saves lives.
COVID Test: We provide RT-PCR, antigen, and antibody tests for COVID. Results are quick, reliable, and travel-approved.
Final & Pre-Employment Medical: We help job seekers and workers with medical clearance reports. Our reports are accepted internationally.
DOFE Online Reporting: Medical reports are uploaded directly to the DOFE system. This speeds up visa processing and avoids manual errors.
Vision, Dental, and Hearing: Our clinic includes eye, dental, and hearing tests. All are done under expert supervision in one place.
X-Ray, ECG & Spirometry: We offer chest X-ray, heart ECG, and lung tests. These help check breathing, heart health, and chest clarity.
Drug & TB Testing: We perform urine drug screening and tuberculosis tests. These are needed for Gulf countries and embassy approvals.
Serology & Blood Testing: We test for HIV, HBsAg, VDRL, HCV, and more. Reports are fast and accurate with modern machines.
Biochemistry & Hematology: We check kidney, liver, sugar, cholesterol, and full blood counts. Reports are available the same day.
Insurance Checkup: For migrant worker insurance claims, we offer all tests. Documents are processed with your medical certificate.
Vaccination: We give required vaccines like Hepatitis B, Typhoid, and Tetanus. Youâ€™ll receive official vaccine proof for travel.
OPD General Services: Visit us for fever, injury, headache, or common issues. We have experienced doctors on daily OPD service.
